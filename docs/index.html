Requirements

Works with iOS 8 or higher and Android 4.3 or higher.
You will also need Bluetooth enabled on your device.
NOTE: Summit works on tablets, but is optimized for phone.

Overview

Summit is the second of the IBM ReadyApp Series. This app seeks to reshape the specialty retail store shopping experience. Open up a direct line of communication with shoppers to personalize their experience. Understand the customer in the context of their journey and focus on real time personalization and engagements.

Using the app, customers can view products available for purchase at a Summit store. Additionally, they can add these items to a list to track the items they wish purchase. While in a Summit store, their lists will automatically reorder depending on which department the user is currently in, moving the items from that department to the top of their list. The different departments are denoted by beacons. Additionally, the app will send push notifications to the user when they are close to a Summit store via Xtify.
